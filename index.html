Mel's Bakery in Jersey City

Mel's Bakery is a neigborhood bakery in Jersey City, baking fresh breads, pastries, and classic treats everyday. We focus on simple, well-made classics that feel familiar and comforting, and worth coming back for.

Located near Liberty State Park, our bakery is an easy stop for locals and visitors alike. Whether you're picking up a loaf of bread, a pastry for the morning, or something sweet to share, we're here to make everyday moments a little better.

We're proud to operate as a no-waste bakery in Jersey City, baking fresh breads, pastries, and classic treats daily. We focus on familiar favorites made well, using simple ingredients and thoughtful processes.
