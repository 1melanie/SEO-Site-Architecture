Menu

Our Menu includes a roatating selection of fresh breads, pastries, cakes, and classic bakery staples, all baked daily. Items may change based on the day's bake and seasonal availability.

Because we focus on freshness and minimizing waste, quantities are intentionally limited. Any remaining baked goods are donated through local partnerships, allowing us to bake generously while operating responsibly.
